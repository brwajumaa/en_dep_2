About

This website is for English department students, we provided the classes and their descriptions in both languages English and Kurdish, the aim of this website is to give students a general idea about each class that they take throughout their semester. And most importantly it help students to be prepared for the classes in term of hard and difficulty. Also for those high-school graduates who want to apply for this major.
